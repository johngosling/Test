Hogan Development Survey Intepretive Reports

 

This facility allows you to generate a report for the Hogan Development Survey™, a personality questionnaire published by Hogan Assessment Systems. The report is prepared by Team Focus and is based upon the percentile scores generated by the Hogan Assessment Systems reports.

In order to use this facility, you must have available a Hogan Assessment Systems report for the HDS which shows the percentile scores for each of the 11 scales. You must also have a Profiling for Success account, the client code and password for which must be entered below.

To generate a report, please enter the details requested below and then click on the Continue button. For each report generated, your Profiling for Success account will be debited by 25 credits.
